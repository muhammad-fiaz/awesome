## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use our website, you may provide us with information in the following ways:

- **Content Submissions:** When you submit a resource, project, tool, or learning material for inclusion on our platform, we collect the information you provide in your submission, including your GitHub username, project name, description, and any other details you choose to include.

- **Communications:** When you contact us via email, GitHub issues, or other channels, we collect the information you provide in your correspondence.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

- **Device Information:** Browser type and version, operating system, device type, screen resolution, and language preferences.
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, scroll depth, navigation paths, and referral sources.
- **Network Information:** Anonymized IP address (truncated to the last octet), internet service provider, and approximate geographic location (country/city level only).
- **Cookie Data:** Information collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies as described in our [Cookie Policy](/awesome/legal/cookie-policy/).

We may receive information from third-party services:

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Data Storage

- **Hosting:** Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which stores static files on GitHub's infrastructure.
- **Analytics Data:** Google Analytics data is stored on Google's servers in accordance with Google's data retention policies.
- **Comment Data:** Comments are stored as GitHub Discussions, managed by GitHub.
- **Local Storage:** Certain preferences (theme, sidebar state) are stored in your browser's local storage and are not transmitted to our servers.

### 3.2 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls to limit unauthorized access to data
- Data minimization practices to collect only what is necessary

### 3.3 Data Retention

- **Analytics Data:** Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months.
- **Comment Data:** Retained indefinitely as part of GitHub Discussions.
- **Content Submissions:** Retained indefinitely as part of our platform's content.
- **Local Storage:** Retained until you clear your browser data.

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit the site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they visited prior to coming to our site.

- **Data Collected:** Anonymized IP addresses, browser type, device type, pages visited, time spent on pages, referral sources.
- **Privacy Policy:** [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Opt-Out:** [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

### 4.2 Google Tag Manager

Google Tag Manager is a tag management system that allows us to manage JavaScript and marketing tags without modifying code directly. GTM itself does not use cookies but may trigger other tags that do.

- **Privacy Policy:** [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 4.3 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

- **Data Collected:** Ad interaction data, interest-based categories, browsing history across participating sites.
- **Privacy Policy:** [Google AdSense Policies](https://support.google.com/adsense/answer/1355411)
- **Ad Settings:** [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com)

### 4.4 Giscus (GitHub Discussions)

Our comment system is powered by Giscus, which uses GitHub Discussions. When you post a comment, your GitHub profile information is displayed publicly.

- **Data Collected:** GitHub username, avatar, public profile, comment content.
- **Privacy Policy:** [GitHub Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)

### 4.5 GitHub Pages

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub Pages collects server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and request timestamps.

- **Privacy Policy:** [GitHub Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)

### 4.6 GitHub Sponsors

If you choose to sponsor our project through GitHub Sponsors, your payment information is processed by GitHub and/or Stripe. We do not have access to your payment details.

- **Privacy Policy:** [GitHub Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)
