## 1. General Refund Policy

### 1.1 Voluntary Donations

All donations and sponsorships made through our Platform are **voluntary contributions** and are generally **non-refundable**. Donations are made to support the development, maintenance, and growth of our open-source platform and community resources.

### 1.2 Exceptional Circumstances

We may, at our sole discretion, provide refunds in the following exceptional circumstances:

- **Duplicate Charges:** If you were charged multiple times for the same donation due to a technical error.
- **Unauthorized Transactions:** If a donation was made without your authorization (e.g., fraudulent access to your account).
- **Technical Errors:** If a technical malfunction caused an incorrect amount to be charged.
- **Platform Not Delivered:** If you made a payment for a specific service that was not delivered as promised.

## 2. Donation Platforms and Refund Eligibility

### 2.1 GitHub Sponsors

Donations made through [GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/muhammad-fiaz) are subject to GitHub's own refund policies:

- **Recurring Sponsorships:** You can cancel your recurring sponsorship at any time. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are provided for the current billing period.
- **One-Time Donations:** One-time donations are generally non-refundable. Refund requests must be directed to GitHub Support.
- **Refund Process:** To request a refund for a GitHub Sponsors donation, please contact [GitHub Support](https://support.github.com) directly.

### 2.2 Other Payment Platforms

Donations made through other platforms (such as [pay.muhammadfiaz.com](https://pay.muhammadfiaz.com)) are subject to the respective platform's refund policies:

- **Platform-Specific Terms:** Each payment platform has its own terms and conditions regarding refunds. Please review the applicable platform's refund policy before making a donation.
- **Processing Fees:** Any processing fees charged by the payment platform are non-refundable.

## 3. Refund Eligibility Criteria

### 3.1 Eligible for Refund

You may be eligible for a refund if:

- You were charged more than the intended donation amount due to a technical error
- A duplicate charge was processed for the same transaction
- A transaction was unauthorized (e.g., unauthorized access to your payment method)
- A paid service was not delivered as described
- You cancel a recurring sponsorship before the next billing cycle

### 3.2 Not Eligible for Refund

The following are generally not eligible for refunds:

- Voluntary one-time donations (except in cases of duplicate charges or unauthorized transactions)
- Recurring sponsorship charges for the current billing period after cancellation
- Donations made more than 60 days ago
- Donations where the donor has received promised benefits or recognition
- Donations made under the influence of alcohol or substances
- Donations made due to a change of heart or financial circumstances

## 4. How to Request a Refund

### 4.1 Contact Us

To request a refund, please contact us through one of the following channels:

- **Email:** [contact@muhammadfiaz.com](mailto:contact@muhammadfiaz.com)
- **GitHub Issues:** [github.com/muhammad-fiaz/awesome/issues](https://github.com/muhammad-fiaz/awesome/issues) (for platform-related issues only)
- **Payment Platform:** Contact the payment platform directly for platform-specific refund requests

### 4.2 Required Information

When requesting a refund, please provide:

- Your name and contact information
- Date and amount of the donation
- Reason for the refund request
- Transaction ID or receipt (if available)
- Any supporting documentation

### 4.3 Response Time

We will acknowledge your refund request within 48 hours and aim to resolve it within 14 business days. Complex cases may require additional time.

## 5. Refund Processing

### 5.1 Refund Method

Refunds will be processed using the original payment method whenever possible. If the original payment method is unavailable, we will work with you to find an alternative solution.

### 5.2 Processing Time

- **Credit/Debit Cards:** 5-10 business days
- **PayPal:** 3-5 business days
- **Bank Transfers:** 7-14 business days
- **Other Methods:** Processing time varies by platform

### 5.3 Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer a partial refund if:

- The full refund amount is not appropriate given the circumstances
- Processing fees have already been incurred
- Benefits or recognition have already been provided

## 6. Cancellation of Recurring Sponsorships

### 6.1 How to Cancel

You can cancel your recurring sponsorship at any time through:

- **GitHub Sponsors:** Visit [github.com/sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors) and manage your active sponsorships
- **Other Platforms:** Contact the respective platform for cancellation instructions

### 6.2 Effect of Cancellation

- Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing period
- You will continue to have access to any benefits for the remainder of the current period
- No partial refunds are provided for the current billing period
- You will not be charged for future periods after cancellation

## 7. Disputed Charges

### 7.1 Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or payment provider, we will:

- Cooperate with your bank or payment provider to resolve the dispute
- Provide documentation and evidence of the transaction
- Request that the chargeback be reversed if the donation was legitimate

### 7.2 Fraudulent Chargebacks

Initiating a fraudulent chargeback (i.e., disputing a legitimate donation) may result in:

- Suspension of your access to our Platform
- Reporting to relevant authorities if fraud is suspected
- Legal action to recover the disputed amount
