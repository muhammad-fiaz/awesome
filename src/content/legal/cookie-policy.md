## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide a better user experience, and supply information to the owners of the site. Cookies may be "session cookies" (which expire when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (which remain on your device until they expire or are deleted).

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation of our website. Without them, certain services you have asked for cannot be provided.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `theme-preference` | Stores your selected theme (dark or light mode) in localStorage | Persistent until deleted |
| `sidebar-state` | Remembers sidebar collapsed/expanded state | Session |
| `cookie-consent` | Records your cookie consent preference | Persistent (1 year) |

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized information about how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information such as how often users visit the site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they visited prior to coming to our site.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique users by assigning a randomly generated number as a client identifier | 2 years |
| `_ga_*` | Google Analytics | Used to persist session state | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique users | 24 hours |
| `_gat` | Google Analytics | Used to throttle request rate | 1 minute |

**Data Collected:** Anonymized IP addresses, browser type, device type, pages visited, time spent on pages, referral sources, and geographic region (country/city level only).

**Opt-Out:** You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### 2.3 Google Tag Manager Cookies

Google Tag Manager (GTM) is a tag management system that allows us to manage JavaScript and marketing tags without modifying code directly. GTM itself does not use cookies but may trigger other tags that do.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_gcl_au` | Google AdSense | Used by Google AdSense to experiment with advertisement efficiency | 3 months |
| `test_cookie` | Google | Checks if the user's browser supports cookies | Session |

**Note:** GTM loads tags from third-party services (such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense) which may set their own cookies. These cookies are governed by the respective third party's privacy policy.

### 2.4 Google AdSense Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |

| `DSID` | Google DoubleClick | Used for targeting purposes to build a profile of the visitor's interests | 2 weeks |

| `__gpi` | Google | Registers a unique ID that identifies the user's device for targeted advertising | 13 months |
| `NID` | Google | Registers a unique ID that identifies the user's device for targeted advertising | 6 months |

**Personalized Ads:** Google and its partners may use cookies to serve personalized advertisements based on your interests. You can manage your ad preferences at [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com).

**Non-Personalized Ads:** If you opt out of personalized advertising, you will still see ads, but they will not be tailored to your interests.

## 5. Your Cookie Choices

You have the following options to manage cookies:

### 5.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:
- Block all cookies
- Accept all cookies
- Delete cookies when you close your browser
- Receive a notification when a cookie is set

### 5.2 Google Analytics Opt-Out

Install the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) to prevent Google Analytics from collecting data about your visits.

### 5.3 Ad Personalization

Manage your ad personalization preferences at [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com). You can also opt out of personalized advertising from participating ad networks at [Your Online Choices](https://www.youronlinechoices.com).

### 5.4 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner. You can accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your preference is stored in localStorage and can be changed at any time.

## 6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable or block cookies, some features of our website may not function correctly:
- **Theme preference** will not be remembered between sessions
- **Sidebar state** will not persist
- **Analytics** will not track your visits (this is optional and does not affect functionality)
- **Ad personalization** will be disabled (you will still see non-personalized ads)
