Today we are thrilled to announce that the Awesome platform has officially crossed a major milestone: listing over 1,000 high-quality developer resources, libraries, and guides. 

This achievement was only possible thanks to the tireless support of our active community. From PR contributors to readers sharing our platform, we owe a huge thank you to everyone.

## What's Next?

Moving forward, we are working on:
- High-performance client-side search indexing with Pagefind.
- Structured publisher schema integration for Google search snippets.
- Interactive, motion-less sidebars for smoother, snappier navigation.
