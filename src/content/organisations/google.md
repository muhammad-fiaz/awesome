Google is an American multinational technology company. It is widely considered one of the Big Five American information technology companies, alongside Amazon, Apple, Meta, and Microsoft.

## Open Source and Developers
Google is a major contributor to the open-source community, maintaining projects like Chromium, Android, Kubernetes, TensorFlow, Go, Angular, Flutter, and many others.
